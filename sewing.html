Sewing

Course Description

The Sewing and Knitting Course is designed to teach participants both basic and advanced skills in the art of sewing and garment making. Whether you are a beginner or have some experience, this course will help you develop your sewing skills, starting from learning the basics like taking measurements and cutting fabrics to advanced techniques such as hand stitching, using a sewing machine, and custom tailoring. The course offers the opportunity to apply what you learn through practical projects aimed at enhancing your understanding and improving your skills.

Objectives:

Equip participants with both basic and advanced skills in sewing and knitting.
Develop the ability to design and create garments and accessories professionally.
Build confidence in handling fabrics, machines, and various sewing tools.

Introduction to Sewing and Knitting

Sewing Basics: An introduction to essential sewing tools and different types of fabrics.
Taking Measurements: How to accurately take measurements for perfect results.
Cutting Fabrics: Learning the correct methods of cutting fabrics to avoid waste.

Getting to Know the Sewing Machine

Operating the Sewing Machine: How to properly set up and use a sewing machine.
Basic Stitches: An overview of basic stitches and how to use them in various projects.
Machine Maintenance: Tips for keeping your sewing machine in good working condition.

Hand Sewing Techniques

Hand Stitches: Learning basic and advanced hand stitches and their applications.
Garment Repair: How to professionally repair tears and defects in clothing.
Edge Finishing: Techniques for neatly and securely finishing edges.

Designing and Making Garments

Pattern Selection: How to choose appropriate patterns and designs.
Garment Making: Steps to design and create garments from start to finish.
Final Touches: Adding final touches like buttons and zippers.

Practical Projects

Garment Sewing Project: Completing a full garment sewing project.
Accessory Design: Learning how to make accessories such as bags and scarves.
Clothing Upcycling: Techniques for turning old clothes into new pieces.
